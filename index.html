about

Lit Lambs

Lit Lambs is a resource hub, providing scripture branded items for children to advance godliness.

We believe in children beholding God's word so much that they start to become the word of God that they behold.

Deliberate children's discipleship from home is our goal.

Also acknowledging that mothers have the responsibility of nurturing their children, we also provide support information and resources to mothers to fulfil effectively the ministry of motherhood.
